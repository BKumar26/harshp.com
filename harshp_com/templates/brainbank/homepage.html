A Bank is a set of similar things. A BrainBank is a collection of ideas.

Often times, when we least expect it, some inspiring idea strikes us. And we dismiss the idea, or forget it owing to the heat of the moment. Later on someday, we think, I had this idea before! If only I had thought of it then. A BrainBank is precisely built to prevent this. It houses all your ideas, no matter how simple, no matter how crazy. Every idea has potential, and every idea has benefits. All it needs is a little introspection. These ideas also serve as an inspiration for future endeavors.
