Bio

Currently a Ph.D researcher with ADAPT research center at School of Computer Science and Statistics of Trinity College Dublin. Started in April 2016 and expected to graduate by April 2020. My research is on how GDPR obligations can be proven based on provenance information, specifically using semantic web technologies. My PhD work is available at OpenScience - ADAPT.
Publications: my website, Google Scholar, dblp.
I also keep a research blog.
